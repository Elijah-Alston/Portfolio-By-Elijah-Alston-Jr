ABOUT US

Background
Founded in 2017 by teenage brothers hoping to earn enough money to buy a car. Da Bomb Brownies was formed by demand for brownies made with our old, family recipe. Our family bakes using all natural ingredients to deliver delicious, chocolaty, moist gourmet brownies in unique epicure flavors. Whether you’re looking for a quick bite or something to bring home and share, we’ve got just the thing for you
